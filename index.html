‚öô Working Principle

The system uses soil moisture sensors to check water levels in the soil. If moisture is below a set threshold, the microcontroller automatically turns ON the pump/valve through a relay. Water is supplied until the soil reaches the required moisture level. The data is sent to an IoT platform so users can monitor and control irrigation remotely.
